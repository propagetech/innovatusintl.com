ABOUT INNOVATUS

Innovatus is a privately held startup, incorporated in Middle East & Africa region with operational reach in Asia Pacific, Europe and North American markets. The brains behind Innovatus have cumulative experience of 100+ man years in the Information Technology industry across various roles. Initial investment for Innovatus was raised through private equity with options for future individual and institutional investments.

Innovatus was established to bridge the gap between growing demand for consultative services and professional delivery. The three pillars of our business are Consulting, Technology & Innovation. Our experienced team have years of experience in Government, BFSI, Education, Manufacturing and ITES industry. We take-on the IT infrastructure service load of organizations so that our customers can focus on their core business. Our services are delivered using well established and proven practices to ensure cost effective, on-time delivery.
At the core of Innovatus is our ethical business values. We partner with our customers to create a trust engagement and commit our services with financially backed service level agreements. This win-win formulae helps create a conducive business relationship and results in 100% customer satisfaction. Our investment in the best available tools and systems to run our internal process helps us differentiates us from our competitors.

Vision

To be a leading professional services organization, with global reach, that will support Information Technology initiative of our customers by providing cost optimized services within defined timelines.

Mission

Ease our customers’ complex IT operational challenges and let them focus on their core business by delivering service excellence in the field of Information Technology through industry standard best practices in service delivery that include consulting, deployment and managed infrastructure services.
